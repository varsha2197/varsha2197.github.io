Defense against DoS in VANET
Aadithya Venkatanarayanan, Anoosha Sagar, Nrithya Theetharappan, Srividhya Balasubramanian, Tyler Davis  |  Fall 2017

We simulated Denial of Service in VANETS where the RSU floods the vehicles with messages denying equal channel access and deployed a fall back RSU to reduce the impact of flooding and used channel switching mechanisms in DSRC to re enable medium access for the cars to communicate using messages, successfully reducing the impact of DoS in VANET
